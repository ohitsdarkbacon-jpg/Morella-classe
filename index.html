Meet your teacher

About Morella

¡Hola! I'm Morella, a native Spanish speaker from Venezuela with a genuine passion for language and teaching. I've spent years helping students at all levels find their voice in Spanish — and I know how to make learning feel natural, not forced.

My lessons are patient, structured, and always adapted to you. I don't believe in one-size-fits-all approaches: if you learn better through conversation, we talk. If you need grammar drilled, we drill. If you want to watch films and discuss them in Spanish, we do that too.

What I care about most is that you leave every class feeling like you actually progressed.
